Privacy Policy

App Name: Outfit Rater

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Outfit Rater ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address to create and manage your account via Supabase Authentication.
  • Photos: You voluntarily upload photos of your outfits for AI rating. These photos are processed and stored to display your rating history.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your GPS location solely to fetch local weather information to personalize outfit suggestions. Location data is never stored.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic usage information (outfit ratings, history) to provide the core functionality of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered outfit ratings and feedback
  • To display your personal outfit history
  • To fetch weather data relevant to your location for outfit tips
  • To authenticate and manage your account

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com). We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Each user's data is isolated and only accessible to them.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – Authentication and database storage
  • Groq AI – AI outfit analysis (photos are sent for processing)
  • Open-Meteo – Weather data (no personal data sent)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the services listed above.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, all your photos, ratings, and account information will be permanently deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

Outfit Rater is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
